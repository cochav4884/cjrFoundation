Programs & Services

The CJR Foundation offers a variety of programs and services to support individuals and families affected by essential tremor. Explore our offerings below.

- Support Groups: Join our local and online support groups to connect with others who understand your journey.
- Educational Workshops: Attend workshops led by medical professionals to learn more about managing essential tremor.
- Advocacy Initiatives: Get involved in advocacy efforts to raise awareness and influence policy.
- Resource Library: Access a wealth of information and resources on essential tremor.
- Financial Assistance: Learn about programs that may help with treatment costs.
- Community Events: Participate in events that bring together the essential tremor community for support and fun.
