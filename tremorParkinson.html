Tremors vs. Parkinson’s Disease

Essential tremor and Parkinson’s disease are different neurological conditions. Tremors in essential tremor are typically action-based, whereas Parkinson’s tremors often occur at rest. Understanding the differences helps patients, families, and caregivers manage symptoms effectively.
