The Charles J. Rousseau Charitable Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting charitable causes and making a positive impact in the essential tremor community.

We believe in the power of giving back and strive to create lasting change through our awareness efforts.

Join us in our mission to make a difference. Together, we can find solutions to make life easier for those who struggle with essential tremor.
