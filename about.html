The CJR Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting charitable causes and making a positive impact in our community. Founded by Charles J. Rousseau, our mission is to provide resources and assistance to those in need, promote education, and foster community development.

We believe in the power of giving back and strive to create lasting change through our various programs and initiatives. Our foundation focuses on areas such as healthcare, education, poverty alleviation, and disaster relief.

Join us in our mission to make a difference. Together, we can create a better future for all.
